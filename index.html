EASY yet OUTSTANDING video and photo editing app for IPhone. Edit your project with Hypee, create the most powerful images and reels. New effects and presets every week!

Hypee is an extremely handy app, you can use it, wherever you want: at public transport, or at home covered in a blanket. You don’t even need a pc or a laptop with you in order to create professional and aesthetically pleasing videos! Just let your inspiration take a lead!

Hypee is perfect for a wide range of social media platforms: TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and many other.


Using Hypee editor you can take advantage of our following features:


Filters and Presets:

  • ⁃ Over 50 different filters that will make your photos and videos shine!
  • ⁃ Over 30 stunning presets to apply to your videos and photos.

Transitions, Overlays, and Visual Effects:

  • ⁃ More than 50 transitions: Swipe, Blur, Fade in Out, Glitch, Slide, etc.
  • ⁃ Over 30 overlays.
  • ⁃ 30 gorgeous visual effects: Glow, Blur, Glitch, VHS, Vintage, and many others!

Texts and Stickers:

  • ⁃ More than 120 trending stickers
  • ⁃ Over 100 customized scripts and text designs.

Music and Sound Effects:

  • ⁃ All trending music is right there!
  • ⁃ Import songs from your library
  • ⁃ Import music from any other video
  • ⁃ Over 200 sound effects - make your video alive!

Format:

  • ⁃ Create a suitable format for your project!
  • ⁃ 5 formats available: 16:9, 4:3, 9:16, 1:1, 3:4

Keyframes:

  • ⁃ Edit the keyframes for stickers, texts, and video itself.
  • ⁃ Create customized effects, transitions

Share:

  • ⁃ Share your photos and videos with the world via TikTok, Likee, Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat.

Hypee is a motivator itself! If you don’t know where to start, or you want to try different styles: just go ahead and play around with Hypee! Just pick up few filters and try out different presets to get inspired to create more!"
